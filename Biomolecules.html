Q21: A man with blood group A, married to a woman with blood group B, had a child with group AB. What does this mean in terms of genetics?

Source: RRB Group-D 27-09-2022 (Shift-III)
Correct! The blood group of the man is A, which means the possible genotypes are 'IA IA' or 'IA i'. Likewise, the blood group of the woman is B, which means the possible genotypes are 'IB IB' or 'IB i'. Hence, A man with blood group A, married to a woman with blood group B, then the possible blood groups are 'A', 'B', 'AB', and 'O' because gene for blood group 'A' and gene for blood group B are both dominant.
