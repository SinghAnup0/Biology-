Q20: Which of the following shows symbiotic relationship?

Correct! Lichen shows symbiotic relationship. The body of lichen is made up of two organisms, which are algae and fungi, respectively. Some people compare lichen to a consortium in which photobiont is attached to mycobiont. This type of relationship gives mutual benefit to both and this type of life is called symbiotic relationship. The lichen symbiosis is thought to be a mutualism, as both the fungi and the photosynthetic partners, called as photobionts, get benefit. The algal component photosynthesize and provide food for the fungus while the fungal component provide protection from the environment by its filament.
